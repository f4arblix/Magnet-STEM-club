About the Club

Magnet STEM Club is a student-run club that helps classmates learn practical time management while working on fun, hands-on STEM challenges. Instead of lectures, we plan small projects, divide tasks, and meet quick deadlines — so time management becomes a skill you actually use.

"Joining the club helped me stop cramming for assignments. Planning together made it easier — and way more fun." — club member

No experience required. All grades welcome. Just bring curiosity and a phone or laptop.

Why Join?

  • Practice realistic planning: break a project into small steps and set deadlines.
  • Accountability: teammates help keep each other on track.
  • Build skills: communication, leadership, and problem solving.
  • Less stress — better results. Learn without the lecture.

Meeting Format (Example)

  1. 10 min: Check-in & quick time-management tip
  2. 30 min: Team planning — divide roles and set mini-deadlines
  3. 30 min: Hands-on build or challenge
  4. 10 min: Share results & reflect (what worked, what to change)

Quick Resources

Starting tips for students:

  • Try the 2-minute plan: write 3 tasks and the first small step for each.
  • Use a shared checklist (Google Docs) for group tasks.
  • Set one mini-deadline per day — small wins build momentum.
