Magnet STEM Club

Building teamwork, creativity, and time management through hands-on projects

The Problem

Many students struggle with time management. Procrastination, missed deadlines, and late nights finishing work create stress and lower grades. This problem affects motivation, mental health, and school performance.

The Solution: Magnet STEM Club

The Magnet STEM Club is a space where students work together on fun, hands-on STEM challenges while learning better time management skills naturally. Instead of forcing schedules, students learn planning and teamwork by doing engaging projects.

How It Works

  • Each meeting includes a short planning session and a quick STEM challenge.
  • Students share their goals and struggles managing time.
  • Team projects teach how to divide tasks and meet deadlines.
  • Members build habits that reduce stress and improve confidence.

Persuasive Approach

Logos: Research shows that students who practice structured time management have higher grades and lower stress levels.

Pathos: Many students feel alone and overwhelmed. This club offers a community that supports and motivates each other.

Ethos: Educators and psychologists agree that teamwork and accountability help students stay on track and develop responsibility.

Join the Club!

We meet every Thursday after school in Room 204. All students are welcome — no experience needed! Bring your curiosity, ideas, and a willingness to learn and grow together.
